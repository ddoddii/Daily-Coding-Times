Why I became a Software Engineer

Programming is somewhat similar to architecture, except my buildings are made of code and won't collapse (hopefully). I can build products just as I imagine them, but with fewer hard hats and safety inspections. I started my journey with AI and data science. But nowadays, my heart beats for backend engineering, where I ensure that everything runs smoothly behind the scenes.

Now, my mission is to deliver real-life products safely to real-life users without any 'Oops, something went wrong' moments. I'm particularly fascinated by databases and serving machine learning models, ensuring they work seamlessly and efficiently. My commitment to hard work drives me to constantly improve and take on new challenges. Ultimately, my dream goal is to become a professional who can solve business problems with code, and enlighten the world with my imagination.
